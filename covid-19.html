Covid 19 update

We are pleased to announce that we are open and able to make appointments at all of our clinics.

What to expect.

Thirsk

  • The rest of the clinic and facilities are currently closed.
  • We ask patients to wait outside the building until their practitioner comes to collect them.
  • To maintain social distancing we ask patients not to arrive too early.
  • On arrival your temperature will be taken with an infra red thermometer.
  • You will be required to wear a mask or facial covering. One will be provided if you do not have one.
  • You will be asked to read and sign a form. this includes questions regarding Covid 19 and a consent form.
